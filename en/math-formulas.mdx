## Formula Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Inline math" icon="code" defaultOpen>
    Use inline math when the expression should stay inside a sentence.

    ```markdown
    Einstein's equation is $E=mc^2$.
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Display math" icon="square-root-variable">
    Use display math for larger formulas that deserve their own block.

    ```latex
    $$
    \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
    $$
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep inline formulas compact. If the expression contains large fractions, matrices, or several aligned steps, switch to display math.
</Tip>

## Calculus Basics

### Limits

$$
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x} = 1
$$

$$
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{e^x - 1}{x} = 1
$$

$$
\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x} = 1
$$

$$
\lim_{x \to \infty}\left(1+\frac{1}{x}\right)^x = e
$$

### Derivatives

$$
f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}
$$

| Function | Derivative |
| --- | --- |
| $c$ | $0$ |
| $x^n$ | $nx^{n-1}$ |
| $e^x$ | $e^x$ |
| $\ln x$ | $\frac{1}{x}$ |
| $\sin x$ | $\cos x$ |
| $\cos x$ | $-\sin x$ |

$$
\frac{d}{dx}[f(x)g(x)] = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)
$$

$$
\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x)) = f'(g(x))g'(x)
$$

### Integrals

$$
\int f(x)\,dx = F(x) + C
$$

| Function | Integral |
| --- | --- |
| $x^n$ | $\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C,\; n \ne -1$ |
| $\frac{1}{x}$ | $\ln|x|+C$ |
| $e^x$ | $e^x+C$ |
| $\sin x$ | $-\cos x + C$ |
| $\cos x$ | $\sin x + C$ |

$$
\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du
$$

$$
\int f(g(x))g'(x)\,dx = \int f(u)\,du
$$

## Linear Algebra

### Vectors and inner products

$$
\|\mathbf{x}\|_2 = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i^2}
$$

$$
\mathbf{x}^T\mathbf{y} = \sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i y_i
$$

$$
\cos\theta = \frac{\mathbf{x}^T\mathbf{y}}{\|\mathbf{x}\|_2\|\mathbf{y}\|_2}
$$

### Matrices

$$
c_{ij} = \sum_{k=1}^{n} a_{ik}b_{kj}
$$

$$
\mathbf{A} =
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\det(\mathbf{A}) = ad - bc
$$

$$
\mathbf{A}^{-1} = \frac{1}{ad-bc}
\begin{bmatrix}
d & -b \\
-c & a
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### Eigenvalues

$$
\mathbf{A}\mathbf{v} = \lambda \mathbf{v}
$$

$$
\det(\mathbf{A} - \lambda \mathbf{I}) = 0
$$

## Probability and Statistics

$$
P(A \mid B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}
$$

$$
P(A \mid B) = \frac{P(B \mid A)P(A)}{P(B)}
$$

$$
\mathbb{E}[X] = \sum_x xP(X=x)
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(X) = \mathbb{E}[(X-\mu)^2]
$$

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$

## Optimization

$$
\theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta \nabla J(\theta_t)
$$

$$
f(x) \approx f(x_0) + f'(x_0)(x-x_0) + \frac{1}{2}f''(x_0)(x-x_0)^2
$$
