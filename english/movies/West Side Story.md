## Musical Form Charged With Social Conflict

*West Side Story* transforms the energy of the Hollywood musical by binding song and choreography to violence, territory, and ethnic tension. The movement is exhilarating, but it is never detached from conflict. Dance becomes an extension of rivalry, youth, desire, and urban pressure.

What gives the film its lasting force is that the romance is never sheltered from its environment. The lovers inherit a world already organized by division, which makes beauty and doom arrive together.

## Why the Film Still Feels Urgent

Its formal boldness comes from treating performance as social expression rather than decoration. Color, gesture, and music intensify the drama instead of softening it. That makes the tragedy feel earned rather than sentimental.

*West Side Story* endures because it proves that the musical can carry both lyricism and confrontation at full strength.
